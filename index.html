The Internet touches every part of our lives -- but too many politicians aren't doing what it takes to protect what makes the Internet great. That’s where we come in.

We will show up where candidates are, organize high-visibility actions and events -- and, yes, throw Internet parties -- to amplify recent open Internet victories, raise awareness of key issues, and make sure no politician wants to be on the wrong side of the Internet.

Are you in?

I want to come to the Internet party!

Internet2016 is a broad coalition that is bringing Internet issues to the forefront of the 2016 election.

We organize high-visibility actions and events, public forums—and parties—to amplify recent victories, raise awareness of key issues, and make the Internet something no politician can afford to ignore. And we’ll encourage Internet users to promote the principles of free speech, access, privacy, choice and openness to candidates and voters, going to the polls with these issues in mind.
