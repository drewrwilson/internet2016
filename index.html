Fall 2015, Everywhere

Join the party for the internet

In the past six months, we have won some of the most historic victories for the free and open web. We successfully defended Net Neutrality and we stopped the Comcast/Time Warner merger. With the US election coming up in 2016, the next 12 months are critical for ensuring that the internet stays free and open for everyone.

We need a Party for the Internet.

So this Fall we are launching The Party for the Internet with parties for the internet, hosted all over the world.
