The Internet touches every part of our lives — but too many politicians aren't doing what it takes to protect what makes it so great. That’s where we come in.

We'll show up where candidates are, organize high-visibility actions and events — and yes, throw Internet parties — to amplify open Internet victories, raise awareness of key issues, and make sure no politician wants to be on the wrong side of the Internet.

Are you in?

I want to come to the Internet party!

The open Internet is so much more than the network itself.

It’s used to change minds and build movements. We need the open Internet to speak for ourselves, earn a living, be entertained, learn what’s happening in our communities and organize for change.

To protect the Internet we have to mobilize and make sure our leaders pay attention to our top priorities.

We are united by the principles of free speech, access, privacy, choice and openness, which we believe need to be the basis of all Internet policies.
